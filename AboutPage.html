Why we make music:


Well, obviously the main reason that we make music is because we like to. If we didn’t enjoy producing music then we wouldn’t be in a band. The main reason is that we like to reach out to people with our music and put them in a good mood. To change someone’s bad day into a good one the ultimate goal that we try to have with our music.

The type of music we make:


We tend to make the type of music that we feel like producing. Creating all genres of music keeps our job fresh and interesting. It can get boring creating one type of song, and using different genres will not only keep us relevant, but it will also bring in new fans. Overall, we make the music that we feel like making.

How the band formed:


The band members met back in highschool. We all had an interest in music and bonded off of that. We would often produce songs just because we could and it was fun. Eventually, someone had the idea of creating a band with all of the music that we made. Soon after that we started producing more songs and started getting more serious with our music.
